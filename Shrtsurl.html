What is ShrtUrls

ShrtUrls is a URL shortening and monetization platform that allows users to generate short links from long URLs. Website owners and publishers can use ShrtUrls to shorten links and share them across social media, emails, blogs, etc.

Project Goals:

As the product manager overseeing the dashboard redesign project for ShrtUrls, our primary objective was to revamp the existing dashboard to address user feedback and industry trends. The overarching goal was to enhance user experience (UX) and usability by modernizing the interface and streamlining access to key features.

Purpose of Redesigning the Dashboard and Desired Outcomes:

The ShrtUrls dashboard, though functional, exhibited an outdated design that hindered user engagement and efficiency. Recognizing the importance of a visually appealing and intuitive interface, our purpose in redesigning the dashboard was twofold: to attract users with an attractive UI and to provide quick access to essential functionalities.
The desired outcomes of the dashboard redesign were as follows:

Improved Usability:

Our aim was to simplify the user experience by reorganizing navigation and surfacing key features prominently, eliminating the need for users to dig through settings or profiles to perform common actions.

Enhanced Visual Design:

By modernizing the visual design, we sought to create an aesthetically pleasing interface that would captivate users and convey a sense of professionalism and trustworthiness..

Streamlined Access to Actions:

We aimed to empower users with quick and efficient access to actions such as link management, analytics, and account settings, reducing friction and enhancing productivity.

Increased User Engagement:

Through a combination of improved usability and attractive UI design, we anticipated a boost in user engagement, with users spending more time on the dashboard and exploring its features.

Positive User Experience:

Ultimately, our goal was to deliver a seamless and delightful user experience that would leave a lasting impression on users, fostering loyalty and advocacy for the ShrtUrls platform.

Page Structure

The redesigned ShrtUrls dashboard was structured into logical sections to help publishers easily navigate between essential tasks and information areas

The main sections included:

  • New Shorten Link: Allows quick creation of shortened links from long URLs.
  • Dashboard: Centralized home screen with customizable widgets and performance summaries.
  • Manage Links: Hub to view, edit, delete existing shortened links and group them into campaigns.
  • Manage Profile: View public profile details and update account information.
  • Payment: Real-time earnings tracker and withdrawal requests.
  • Manage Payout: History of processed payments and wallet balances.
  • My Account: Sub-sections for personal settings customization.
  • Setting: Options to modify dashboard layout preferences.

User Interface Design:

At Viveprinting, our approach to crafting the visual design of the website is guided by a commitment to elegance, clarity, and user-centricity. We meticulously curated a design language that seamlessly blends functionality with aesthetic appeal, ensuring an intuitive and delightful user experience.

Typography:

We carefully selected two primary fonts, Montserrat and Poppins, to establish a harmonious typographic hierarchy across the website. Montserrat exudes a sense of modernity and sophistication, while Poppins offers excellent readability and versatility. Through strategic use of font weights and styles, we maintain consistency and visual coherence across all textual elements.

Color Scheme:

Our color palette revolves around the warm, inviting hue of #CEA752, evoking feelings of warmth, trust, and professionalism. This golden undertone infuses vibrancy and personality into the design, while also ensuring accessibility and legibility. Complemented by neutral tones and subtle gradients, our color scheme strikes a balance between visual impact and usability.

Overall Style:

The overall style of the website is characterized by its clean, minimalist aesthetic and refined design elements. We prioritize simplicity and clarity in our layout compositions, allowing users to navigate effortlessly and focus on the content. Thoughtful spacing, crisp lines, and subtle animations enhance the user journey, creating a sense of fluidity and coherence.

Key UI Elements:

Throughout the website, users encounter a variety of meticulously crafted UI elements designed to optimize usability and engagement. From sleek navigation menus and intuitive search bars to interactive buttons and form fields, each element is thoughtfully positioned and styled to facilitate seamless interaction.

Development

During the development phase of the Vive Printing website accessible at viveprinting.co.uk , our team meticulously implemented various technologies and coding languages to ensure optimal performance and functionality. Leveraging WordPress as the foundational platform, we employed a combination of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP to bring the website to life. One of the key focuses during development was maintaining pixel-perfect precision and adhering to the design specifications outlined in our collaborative efforts with the UX/UI designers. Through close collaboration with the development team, we ensured seamless integration of the design elements, including typography, color schemes (utilizing #CEA752 as the primary color), and the overall style. Furthermore, our development process prioritized user experience and search engine optimization (SEO) considerations. We held regular meetings with both the SEO team and the development team to align on best practices and implement strategies to enhance website visibility and performance.
