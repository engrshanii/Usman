mycustompackaging

Mycustompackaging.com engaged us to redesign their existing website to improve usability issues and modernize the customer experience. As a leading provider of custom packaging solutions, having an intuitive and visually appealing website is crucial to their business.

Goals:
Improve findability of products and information Streamline purchasing process Establish cohesive brand identity Enhance visual design for modern look and feel
Approach:
Conducted heuristic analysis of existing site to identify usability pain points Performed competitor benchmarking to analyze best practices Created user personas and customer journey maps to focus redesign Developed information architecture and redesigned workflows Designed high-fidelity responsive visual mockups tailored to user goals Implemented UI using HTML, CSS and JavaScript based on approved designs
Results:
The redesigned website elevates the user experience through:
Improved navigation and findability More intuitive category structure Seamless cross-device responsiveness Appealing visual brand identity Increased conversion rate performance The new site establishes mycustompackaging.com as an innovative leader in packaging solutions with a focus on customer experience. The user-centered design provides a seamless journey across devices. Beautiful aesthetics establish cohesive brand identity. The data-driven approach resulted in a design optimized for site metrics.
