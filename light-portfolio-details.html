Vive Printing UK

Viveprinting.co.uk, a printing solutions company, required a website redesign to keep pace with customer expectations. Their existing site suffered from an outdated look and feel, cluttered layouts, and findability issues.
Our UX research revealed opportunities to improve the information architecture and streamline the purchase process. We conducted stakeholder interviews, analyzed site metrics, and reviewed competitor sites to inform the redesign.
Next, we created user personas representing target customers. Their goals and behaviors shaped the sitemap, workflows and wireframes we designed. Our aim was crafting intuitive navigation and interactions tailored specifically to customer needs.
For the visual experience, we focused on conveying Vive Printing's expertise through dynamic, robust imagery while applying minimalist aesthetics for clean layouts. Compelling and informative content establishes them as thought leaders.
The result is a beautifully redesigned website at viveprinting.co.uk that elevates their brand identity through a modern, user-centric experience. Feedback from customer testing validates the highly engaging and usable new design.
The reimagined site architecture, aesthetics, and content strategy re-establishes Vive Printing as an innovative industry leader. Their transformed digital presence effectively showcases their capabilities to prospects across devices.
